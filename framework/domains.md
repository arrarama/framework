# FinOps Domains

FinOps Domains represents a sphere of activity or knowledge. Every organization adopting FinOps will perform activities in all FinOps Domains. Each FinOps Domain contains [Capabilities](/framework/capabilities/) which outline the functional activities performed as part of that Domain. Domains roughly align with the FinOps [lifecycle phases](/framework/phases/). 

- [Allocate](allocate)
- [Report](report)
- [Forecast](forecast)
- [Usage Optimization](usage-optimization)
- [Rate Optimization](rate-optimization)
- [Organizational Alignment](organizational-alignment)
